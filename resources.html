Interactive Learning Paths

Progress saved locally. Estimated times account for Ethiopian internet/power constraints.

Ethiopian Web Developer

6-12 months
  1. HTML/CSS/JS Basics (freeCodeCamp – offline)
  2. React.js (most in-demand in Addis jobs)
  3. Node.js/Express backend
  4. Deploy on local hosts (HahuCloud/Yegara)
  5. Integrate payments (Chapa, HelloCash)
  6. Amharic localization & offline support
30% Complete

Mobile App Developer

8-14 months
  1. Flutter (cross-platform, low data usage)
  2. Android focus (90%+ market share)
  3. Offline-first architecture
  4. Amharic/Ge'ez script support
  5. Publish on local app stores
20% Complete

AI & Data Science Track

9-15 months
  1. Python basics (Anaconda offline)
  2. ML with Google Colab (low bandwidth)
  3. Local apps: AgriTech, Amharic NLP
  4. Ethiopian AI Institute projects
25% Complete
